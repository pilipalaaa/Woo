Great architecture In China

The Great Wall, also known as the Great Wall, is a military defense project in ancient China. It is a tall, strong and continuous long Wall, which is used to isolate the enemy. The Great Wall is not a simple isolated Wall, but a defense system with the Wall as the main body and a large number of city barriers and pavilions
